How To Play:

Guess a 5 letter word and enter it via the onscreen keyboard or via your physical keyboard.

Then click "ENTER"

If the word you entered is valid, you will be given the following feedback:

Green: The letter is in the word of the day and in the correct position

Blue: The letter is in the word of the day but is in the wrong position

Grey: The letter is not in the word of the day

You have 6 opportunities to guess the correct 5-letter word. Good luck!
