Multiplayer Information:

There are two options to choose from:

Option 1: Two Player Game - Guessing the word of the day

One player needs to start the game by clicking on "CREATE 2 Player Game". This will generate a unique game ID.
This player should then share the game ID with one other person.
The second player must click on "JOIN a Game" and enter the Game ID they have recieved from the other player
Both players must try and guess the word of the day

Option 2: Three Player Game -Guessing a word chosen by one of the players

One player needs to start the game by entering a 5-letter word of their choice and clicking on "CREATE 3 Player Game". This will generate a unique game ID.
This player should then share the game ID with two other person.
The second and third player must click on "JOIN a Game" and enter the Game ID they have recieved from the first player
Both players must try and guess the word the first player has generated

Once a Multi-player game has began:

Each player must guess a 5 letter word and enter it via the onscreen keyboard or via your physical keyboard.
Click Enter
If the word you entered is valid, you will be given feedback:

Green: The letter is in the word of the day and in the correct position

Blue: The letter is in the word of the day but is in the wrong position

Grey: The letter is not in the word of the day

The first player to correctly guess the word WINS!!!.
